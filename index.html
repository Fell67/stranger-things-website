About the Series

Genre: Science fiction, Horror, Mystery, Drama

Setting: 1980s Hawkins, Indiana

Core Themes:

  • Friendship and loyalty: The basis of the show is the friendship between the main characters and them working together to overcome challenges.
  • Family: The show has examples of several forms of family. From family that shares blood to family created by the group of friends.
  • Love: Different types of love are exhibited in the show, including romantic love, familial love, and the platonic love between friends.
  • Good vs. Evil: The main characters constantly fight against supernatural threats and human antagonists.
  • Courage and resilience: Characters repeatedly face their fears and persevere through traumatic events.

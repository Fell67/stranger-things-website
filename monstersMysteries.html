Monsters and Mysteries

From the depths of the Upside Down come the creatures that haunt Hawkins. Learn about their origins, powers, and the secrets that connect them all.

Creatures of the Upside Down

Demogorgon

The first terror from the Upside Down. A humanoid beast with a flowered head of teeth, hunting by scent and sound.

The Mind Flayer

A hive-mind entity that has the power to control countless creatures and people through psychic domination. It has the ability to manipulate lesser creatures and possess those it sinks its psychic tendrils into. Its goal: to merge the Upside Down and Hawkins together into chaos.

Demodogs

These creatures are adolescent Demogorgons, and just as deadly. They're vicious, loyal, and incredibly fast, able to chase down prey of any size. They move in packs and can easily overwhelm prey through sheer numbers and agility.

Vecna

A dark god of the Upside Down. He uses the fears, guilt, and trauma of Hawkins' residents, shaping their nightmares into deadly daytime visions. Framed as the ultimate evil that the heroes of Hawkins have to face, Vecna has twisted the Upside Down to his will, and aims to spread his control into reality through Hawkins.

Monsters and Mysteries

Monsters of the Upside-Down

Demogorgon

The Original Terror of Hawkins

A humanoid beast with a flowered head of teeth, hunting by scent and sound. Moving by night, this creature began it's reign of terror by dragging young Hawkins local, Will Byers, into the Upside-Down.

The Mind Flayer

The Monsterous Hive-Mind

An interdimensional entity that has the power to control countless creatures and people through psychic domination. It manipulates lesser creatures and those it possesses through psychic tendrils, seeking to merge the Upside Down and Hawkins into chaos.

Demodogs

Pack Hunters of the Upside-Down

These creatures are adolescent Demogorgons, and no less deadly than their adult counterparts. They're fast, vicious, and loyal to the Mind Flayer as their leader. They hunt in packs and overwhelm their prey with sheer numbers and agility.

Vecna

The Sinister Mastermind of The Upside-Down

A dark god of the Upside-Down who twists the fears and guilt of Hawkins’ residents into deadly visions. He seeks to merge his realm with reality, spreading his reach from the Upside-Down into the real world, staring with Hawkins.

Science Behind the Strange

Deep within the quiet town of Hawkins, Indiana, the Hawkins National Laboratory conducted government-funded research that delved far beyond the ordinary. What began as scientific exploration soon touched on the extraordinary—experiments involving perception, consciousness, and dimensions unseen by most.

Though the lab’s work was meant to stay secret, its discoveries opened doors that were never meant to be opened, allowing forces from elsewhere to slip through. The strange phenomena that followed would forever change the lives of those who called Hawkins home.
